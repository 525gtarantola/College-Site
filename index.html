Welcome to Temple University

Overview

Welcome to Temple University, a prestigious public research institution located in the heart of Philadelphia. Our mission is to provide access to a transformative education that empowers students to succeed in a diverse and dynamic world. We value innovation, inclusivity, and a commitment to lifelong learning.

Location: 1801 N Broad St, Philadelphia, PA 19122
Contact: (215) 204-7000

As an incoming freshman, you are about to embark on a transformative journey filled with opportunities for personal and academic growth. Choosing Temple means exploring, learning, and innovating alongside talented students, researchers, and experts from around the world. Most of all, it means joining a global community known for its insatiable grit, passion, and perseverance.

Academic Programs

At Temple University, we offer a diverse range of academic programs across various fields. Our available majors include:

  • Business, Management, Marketing, and Related Support Services
  • Health Professions and Related Programs
  • Communication, Journalism, and Related Programs
  • Visual and Performing Arts
  • Biological and Biomedical Sciences
  • Psychology
  • Computer and Information Sciences and Support Services
  • Engineering
  • Social Sciences
  • Parks, Recreation, Leisure, Fitness, and Kinesiology

Our curriculum is designed to provide hands-on learning experiences, with course requirements tailored to equip students for success in their chosen fields. Students benefit from a low student-faculty ratio of 12:1, ensuring personalized attention and support.

Student Life

At Temple University, student life is vibrant and engaging. Our campus offers a variety of facilities and amenities designed to enhance your university experience, including state-of-the-art recreational centers, libraries, and study lounges.

Students can join a diverse range of organizations and clubs that cater to various interests, from academic and professional groups to cultural and recreational clubs. These organizations foster a sense of community and provide opportunities for leadership and personal development.

Social and extracurricular activities abound, with events throughout the year, including festivals, concerts, and sports games. These activities help you connect with fellow students and create lasting memories.

Housing options are plentiful, ranging from traditional dormitories to apartment-style living. Temple offers competitive pricing, ensuring that students can find a suitable and affordable place to call home during their studies.

Financial Aid and Costs

At Temple University, we understand that financing your education is a crucial part of your academic journey. Our tuition and fees are competitive, ensuring you receive a high-quality education without breaking the bank.

We offer various financial aid options to support our students, including scholarships, loans, and grants. Scholarships are available based on merit and need, helping to reduce your overall educational expenses. Additionally, federal and state loan programs provide flexible financing options to make college more affordable.

To help manage costs, Temple offers payment plans that allow you to spread tuition payments over the semester. It's important to stay informed about deadlines for financial aid applications and tuition payments to ensure you secure the best options available.

Alumni Success

Temple University boasts a distinguished list of notable alumni who have made significant contributions across various fields. From influential leaders in business to award-winning artists and public figures, our alumni exemplify the spirit of excellence fostered at Temple.

Our graduates enjoy impressive career outcomes, with high job placement rates that reflect the quality of education and training they receive. Many alumni have successfully launched their careers in competitive industries, thanks to the skills and knowledge gained at Temple.

Temple University maintains strong partnerships with various industries, providing students with valuable internship opportunities. These partnerships not only enhance learning but also facilitate professional networking, helping students make important connections before they graduate.
