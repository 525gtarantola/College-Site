About Us

Introducing The Little Bird Toy Company, where imagination takes flight! Nestled in the heart of a quaint small town, our family-owned business, led by siblings Evan, Abigail, and Elizabeth, has been crafting enchanting wooden toys for children for over three years. With a steadfast commitment to quality craftsmanship and nurturing young minds, we've cultivated a devoted local following.

Now, inspired by the support of our cherished community and fueled by our passion for creativity, we're spreading our wings. The Little Bird Toy Company is soaring into the digital realm, eager to share our heirloom-quality treasures with families across the nation. Join us on our journey as we bring the magic of handcrafted toys to homes far and wide.
